Djerba Traditional Heritage Musuem

This interesting little museum is where to come if you want to dig a little deeper under Djerba's resort skin.

Although small, the galleries here display an impressive number of exhibits, all focused on Djerba's local heritage and exploring and preserving the island's cultural traditions.

As well as displays focused on the history of the island, there are well-curated exhibits devoted to Djerba's Jewish heritage, and fascinating displays of vibrant, traditional costumes, beautiful old jewelry, antique ceramics, and extravagantly carved wedding chests. The museum is housed next door to the beautiful 18th-century Zaouia of Sidi Zitouni. Visitors can also enter this mausoleum to view its impressive stalactite-designed ceilings.
