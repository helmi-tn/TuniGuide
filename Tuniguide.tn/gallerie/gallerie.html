Most Polpular Cites In Tunisia


Chott El Djerid

It is the largest salt pan of the Sahara Desert, with a surface area of over 7,000 km2 (some sources state 5,000 km2).

One of the most surreal sightseeing attractions in Tunisia, the Chott el Djerid is a long day trip from Djerba (290 kilometers) but worth it for anyone who wants to visit this bizarre natural formation.

Djerba Explore Park

Djerba Explore Park is situated in the middle of the Midoun tourist area, near Taguermess lighthouse.

Djerba Explore Park sits on the island's coastal road, 20 kilometers east of Houmt Souk. The complex combines the Lalla Hadria Museum and the Djerba Explore Crocodile Farm.

Djerba Traditional Heritage Museum

Occupying a curvaceous purpose-built structure next to the Zaouia of Sidi Zitouni about 200m from the town centre, it houses expertly curated and displayed exhibits on traditional Djerban life and culture.

Onk Jemel (Mos Espa)

Star Wars fans: Take a deep breath now. Out in the desert, in the area known as Onk Jemel, near Tozeur, is the abandoned film set of Mos Espa surrounded by mammoth dunes.

This is where Anakin Skywalker did his first pod racing in Episode 1 - The Phantom Menace.
