RESTAURANTS IN DJERBA

The national dish is rice and curry, traditionally eaten with your fingers. Cutlery is provided for foreigners. The meal consists of six or seven plates of fiery meat, fish and chicken served with heaps of rice accompanied by side dishes of yoghurt and cucumber and vegetable curries. Roadside stalls also dish up snacks like rotis (pancakes) and malu pan (spicy meat or fish rolls).




EL FERIDA   +216 98 281 108

This restaurant and casino offers entertainment and fun for the whole evening. Drop by at the bar, indulge yourself in tasty food at the restaurant or play a round of roulette at the casino.

RACHID ET SOFIE  +216 20 946 915

Located in the city of Midoun, this restaurant offers you traditional Tunisian cuisine with a modern twist. Come by to enjoy generous servings for reasonable prices.

CHOUCHOU  +216 25 919 349

Chouchou is a warm and welcoming restaurant well known for its friendly staff, kind owner and fresh food. They've received praise for their couscous.

HAROUN   +216 75 650 488

What separates Haroun from other restaurants in Djerba is the location. It is beautifully situated on the water's edge and is a replica of a boat deck.

RESTAURANT SIDI ALI   +216 21 805 506

Located directly at the beach, this restaurant is the ideal place to drop by after a long day of sunbathing and swimming. Enjoy fresh fish and seafood on the menu and why not try one of their delicious cocktails?
