MONASTIR

Monastir, also called Mistīr , is a city on the central coast of Tunisia , in the Sahel area, some 20 kilometres (12 miles) south of Sousse and 162 kilometres (101 miles) south of Tunis. Traditionally a fishing port, Monastir is now a major tourist resort . Its population is about 93,306. It is the capital of Monastir Governorate . Monastir was founded on the ruins of the Punic–Roman city of Ruspina . The city sits in the northeast of Tunisia, on its central coast.
