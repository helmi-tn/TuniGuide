RESTAURANTS IN TOZEUR

Discover Restaurants offering the best Tunisian food in Tozeur, Tozeur Governorate, Tunisia. Berber spice. Asian fire. Mediterranean freshness. Anantara Sahara Tozeur Resort & Villas, home to some of Tunisia's best restaurants, offers you the world on a plate in a beautiful desert landscape.




SARAB   +216 70 100 800

Start each day with a sumptuous buffet of coffee and juice, freshly baked pastries and with an open kitchen preparing food live in your favorite restaurant among all of Tozeur.

OASIS  +216 70 100 800

Take a lounge chair to bask or sit on the terrace. Spoil yourself with freshly baked pasta, risotto and pizza. Quench your thirst with a selection of beers, wines and cocktails served at your favorite pool bar in Tunisia.

CHOUCHOU  +216 25 919 349

Chouchou is a warm and welcoming restaurant well known for its friendly staff, kind owner and fresh food. They've received praise for their couscous.

TOZOROUS   +216 95 603 783

This recommended restaurant serves up pasta, grilled meat and couscous dishes in a brick-walled dining room complete with a working coffee pot–shaped fountain in the centre.

MEKING   +216 70 100 800

Travel through the aromas and spices of the Mekong basin, from southern China down to Vietnam and Thailand. Contemporary decor at this Asian restaurant in Tunisia sets the stage for an exotic feast for the senses. Take a seat inside or out on the terrace overlooking the pool oasis.
