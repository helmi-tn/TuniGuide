TOZEUR

Nearly in every coffee shop in Tozeur you can order all kinds of tea,shishas and cocktails. Cafés are known for their tropical vibe view where you can enjoy your time and rest your mind in the afternoon after a long hot weather day.






SAHARA LOUNGE

Set in the palmeraie, this is a great place for a shisha under the palm trees, or a coffee. Courting couples and families sitting at the tables spread throughout the garden.

LA GROTTE   +216 50 191 091

A step up from the street-side cafes, La Grotte, à la its name, is fitted out to look like the inside of a cave, albeit a cool, air-conditioned one. It tends to draw a younger crowd of men.

LE PETIT PRINCE   +216 76 452 518

Previously a restaurant, now a smoky local boozer, this is the best place to get a drink outside the hotel bars in the Zone Touristique.
